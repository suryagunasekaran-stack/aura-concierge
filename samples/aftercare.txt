AURA Medical Aesthetics — General Aftercare Guide

Use this for basic post-treatment care questions.

General (all facial treatments)
- Avoid direct sun and wear SPF 50+ daily for at least one week.
- Do not pick, scratch, or rub treated skin.
- Use a gentle cleanser and moisturiser — no scrubs for 3–5 days unless your therapist says otherwise.
- Skip retinol, acids (AHA/BHA), and strong vitamin C for 48–72 hours after most facials and peels.
- Stay hydrated and avoid heavy sweating (sauna, hot yoga) for 24 hours.

HydraFacial
- Skin may look slightly pink for a few hours — this is normal.
- Makeup can usually be applied the same day if skin is comfortable.
- Continue daily sunscreen.

Chemical peel / Emerald Peel

Before your peel (preparation)
- Stop retinol, strong acids (AHA/BHA), and exfoliating scrubs 2–3 days before.
- Avoid sun exposure and tanning; use SPF 50+ daily in the week before.
- Arrive with a clean face, no makeup if possible.
- Tell us about pregnancy, recent procedures, allergies, or active breakouts when booking.

After your peel (aftercare)
- Expect mild flaking or dryness for 3–7 days depending on peel strength.
- Do not peel off flaking skin — let it shed naturally.
- No waxing, laser, or other active treatments on the same area for 2 weeks.
- Avoid direct sun and wear SPF 50+ daily.

HIFU (face / neck lift)
- Mild warmth, tightness, or small bumps along treatment lines can occur for 1–2 weeks.
- Avoid heat (sauna, steam, hot showers) for 24 hours.
- No facial massage or aggressive exfoliation for 7 days.
- Results build over 8–12 weeks as collagen remodels.

Laser / IPL
- Treated spots may darken briefly before flaking off — do not pick.
- Strict sun avoidance and SPF 50+ for 2 weeks.
- No swimming or gym for 24 hours if skin is warm or red.

When to contact the clinic
- Severe pain, blistering, spreading redness, or fever after a treatment.
- Rash or swelling that worsens after 48 hours.

Contact AURA during clinic hours or via WhatsApp concierge for aftercare questions.
